FAQ

Questions
worth asking

Part of being successful is asking the right questions and listening to the answers.

How can I start earning money? +
Login or register on the Funngro app. Go to the Projects section and choose an easy project — a survey, registration task, or app install. Complete the steps correctly and submit your proof. Once approved, money is credited instantly to your Funngro Wallet. Withdraw directly to your bank account or UPI anytime.
Why should teens work during school? +
Start early, gain independence, and learn while you earn. Most importantly, you get real world experience no classroom can replicate. Work on projects matching your interests and find your true passion before making a final career decision.
What kind of work can I do on Funngro? +
Funngro has 12 project categories: Social Media, Video Creation, Website Design, Influencer Marketing, App Development, Campus Ambassador, Research & Survey, Data Entry, Voice Over, Content Writing, Graphic Design, and Testing. Register for the categories of your choice directly in the app.
How do I get paid? +
Earnings are credited to your Funngro Wallet after your submitted project is approved. You can withdraw directly to your bank account or UPI. There is no minimum withdrawal threshold for most projects.
Is Funngro free to use? +
Yes — registering and using Funngro as a teenlancer is completely free. Download the app, create your profile, and start applying to projects with zero cost, zero subscription.
