Questions before
you post your first project

Everything a decision-maker needs to know before hiring on Funngro.

How do we start working with teenlancers? +
Login to Funngro, add your project brief, select a teenlancer from our curated shortlist, and start working. The entire setup takes under 10 minutes. You'll have applications in your inbox the same day.
How does Funngro find the right talent for us? +
From a pool of 50 lakh teenlancers, we curate shortlists based on their category experience, past project quality, ratings, and commitment scores. You only see candidates who are genuinely ready to deliver your brief — not the entire pool.
Are there any legal requirements for working with teens? +
Yes — you can only work with teens aged 14 and above. Funngro verifies age and requires parental approval before any teenager can apply to your projects. This is handled entirely by us — you don't need to manage any of that compliance yourself.
What if the work quality doesn't meet our standards? +
Payment is only released after you approve the delivered work. If the output doesn't meet your brief, you can request revisions or escalate to the Funngro team. You're never obligated to pay for work you haven't accepted.
Can we run multiple projects simultaneously? +
Absolutely — most companies on Funngro run several projects in parallel across different categories. You can have a teenlancer managing your social media, another writing blogs, and a third doing graphic design all at once.
